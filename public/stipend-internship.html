Stipend Based Industrial Training in Mohali

Tech Engineer Mohali offers 6 Months/3 Months/6 weeks stipend based Industrial training for B.Tech (All Branches)/MCA/M.Sc (IT)/Diploma and other graduate students. Students, who are interested to get stipend based or Free training and 100% placement assistance with Free Spoken English, Personality Development and Interview Preparation (HR+Technical Round) classes, are required to register and enroll for Aptitude-cum-Technical Test.
